What is a hobby of music?

The hobby of music is much more than finding something to give background noise or "finding a vibe". Of course, finding a vibe, and depending on what you are looking for in that moment is a good start. To me, it is about what the artist is trying to convey to the listener, through the instrumentation, the hook, the counter-melody, and timing. This is just the start of it, there is so much to find when you endulge yourself in music. It can start simple, like listening to music on a website like youtube, or using a service like Spotify or Apple Music. Then you want better headphones; or maybe you like earbuds so you get some nice IEMs. These headphones require moe power, so you get an amp, then a DAC, new headphones, a physical equalizer, and next thing you know you have a whole setup!

Getting started in this hobby is really easy, we listen to music all the time! Whether it is while shopping, driving to and from work/school, at a party, a get together and whatever else you can think of. Us as humans love music, and we all have different taste in music. Go and listen to something you would'nt normally listen to, and you might just find your next favorite song, artist, or genre! It only goes up from there, and it stays fresh and interesting the whole way through.

Why Music?

Why do we enjoy music?

Music is something that we enjoy, because of the emotional connection we have to it. Whatever artist, album, genre, or culture the songs come from, we all react and feel a certain way when listening to music. We are surrounded by music all the tiem, and it makes sense because of its application everywhere in our lives. Whether it be when shopping, in the car, out on a walk/jog, at a party, on the phone, and of course the elevator. A lot of music we listen to really is just used as background noise, like the shopping and driving in a car example. It keeps us from feeling the environment we are in is too quiet, since being in a quiet place for too long will make us feel uneasy. Depending on the kind of music you listen to, can also affect how productive you are as well. I am sure a lot of us has a playlist dedicated purely for homework, since that playlist allows us to focus more on the work more than the music and our surroundings.

What about some others?

There are people that simply cannot find much enjoyment from music because of some emotional complexity that does not allow them to empathize with music like the vast majority of people. This condition is called Musical Anhedonia, where they cannot find any satisfaction or benefit from listening to music. This affects some more than others, and is a reason why some feel suppressed emotions compared to other, if any at all.

How do I get started?

How do you want to listen to music?

As said on the homepage, albeit quite short, what kind of audio equiptment are we interested in? There is a lot to choose from, so let's start with a few ideas.

  1. Headphones: This can range from simple dollar store cans, to reference studio headphones. This is the most broad category, and is always evolving.
  2. In Ear Monitors (IEM): Just like headphones, they can range from ones at the dollar store, to crazy expensive reference monitors used for their much increased sensitivity and accuracy.
  3. Bluetooth Speakers: The most convenient when wanting to play music out loud. Let's be honest, phone speakers kind of suck, they either sound bad, aren't loud, or both. This is an easy and fun way to listen to music with a group.
  4. Hi-Fi Stereo: This is getting high end now. Hi-Fi is a step above lossless audio quality, allowing you to hear the most intricate details you would normally not hear.(Most streaming services cannot do lossless, let alone Hi-Fi)
  5. Dedicated Room: This is where you might be insane. Having an entire room in your house dedicated just to listening to music is a step above. This can be done in tandem with the Hi-Fi stereo setup, and you go even further by using foam panels to reduce echo, and tune the audio to make the room sound the best it can be.
